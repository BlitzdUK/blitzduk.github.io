Breakfast

 

 

The Chadwick House Hotel in Macclesfield offers a free full English breakfast to all guests.

The breakfast is served in the hotel's dining room from 7:00am to 9:00am or to your room daily.

The breakfast buffet includes a variety of hot and cold items, such as:

 

Toast

Cereal

Yogurt

Fruit

Eggs

Bacon

Sausages

Hash browns

Baked beans

Tea

Coffee

Juice

 

Guests can also order cooked-to-order items, such as pancakes, waffles, and french toast.

The breakfast buffet is a great way to start your day and fuel up for exploring Macclesfield and the surrounding area.

If you're looking for a comfortable and affordable hotel in Macclesfield with a free breakfast,

The Chadwick House Hotel is a great option!

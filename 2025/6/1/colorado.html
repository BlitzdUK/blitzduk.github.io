Terror Attack at Pearl Street Mall

Published: 1 June 2025

Photo by Kent Kanouse via Flickr

At approximately 14:00 local time on 1 June 2025, a terror incident struck Pearl Street Mall in Boulder, Colorado. Dozens of civilians gathered for a community event were forced to flee when an explosion occurred near the main square.

Authorities quickly established a 150-metre exclusion zone around the blast site. Blitzd Media advises all individuals to use the location check tool above to assess their proximity, and leave immediately if inside the danger area while safely assisting others where possible.

The exclusion zone covers key parts of the mall, local cafés, and public spaces. Emergency services remain on site and investigations are ongoing. Official statements urge citizens to remain clear of the exclusion zone until further notice.

Follow Blitzd Media for continuous updates and verified reports as the situation develops.

Key Information
  • Date: 1 June 2025
  • Location: Pearl Street Mall, Boulder CO
  • Injuries: Multiple
  • Exclusion Zone: 150m radius
